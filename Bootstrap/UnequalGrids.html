The Important Role of Science

🦠 🔍🧪🔬

Science plays an important role in helping people understand the world around them. Through observation, experiments, and research, scientists discover how natural processes work, from the smallest cells to the largest planets.
One major benefit of science is its impact on technology and medicine. Scientific discoveries have led to inventions like computers, smartphones, and vaccines.
Science is also important for the future of the planet. By studying climate, ecosystems, and energy sources, scientists help societies find solutions to environmental problems.
