INDIAN SCHOOL BOUSHAR


Indian School Bousher is located at Plot no: 365, Al Awabi, Al Ansab, Muscat, Sultanate of Oman.
Indian School Bousher has over 3000 students.
Indian School Bousher offers various academic streams for Grade XI, including Science, Commerce, and Humanities, with specific subject choices within each. Indian School Bousher is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE).
